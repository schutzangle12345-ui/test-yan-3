The Pillars of Our Approach

90-Day Value

We reject multi-year experiments that lack interim proof points. Every roadmap begins with a high-impact pilot designed to show ROI within 90 days.

P&L Linkage

If an AI initiative doesn't tie directly to revenue growth or cost savings, it shouldn't exist. We define simple KPIs that link AI work to the bottom line.
